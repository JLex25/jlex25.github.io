World of runeterra

  1. Game
  2. World

Runeterra is a plane of existence composed of the physical realm: an oblate spheroid planet and the Spirit Realm. Runeterra is part of the larger Runeterra Prime universe. The currently known continents the planet comprises are Valoran, Shurima, and the Camavoran Continent. It also boasts several large bodies of water, such as the currently known 18 Seas, and 2 Oceans with several archipelagos and islands around them. The planet orbits around a G-type star while a natural satellite moon orbits around the planet. Runeterra's sun and moon also even mirrored equivalents in the spirit realm.
