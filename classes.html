Classes of Runeterra

  1. Game
  2. Classes

Runaterra is a magic place with a variety of classes you can choose.

Mage

Champions who typically possess great reach, ability-based area of effect damage and crowd control.

Rogue

Fragile but agile damage-focused melee champions that look to swiftly take down their targets.

Fighter

Short-ranged combatants who excel at both dealing and surviving damage.

Marksman

He is a shooter who manages to do the most damage in the shortest time.

Tank

Tanks are melee champions who trade damage for powerful crowd control.

Specialist

A diverse group of champions who do not "fit into a neat little box" in regards to other class/subclass specifications.
